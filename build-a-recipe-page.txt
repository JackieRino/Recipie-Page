      <h1>Fried Rice Recipe</h1>
      <p>Lets create this personalized fried rice with everyday ingredients!</p>

<h2>Ingredients</h2>
  <ul>
    <li> Precooked rice 2 cups</li>
    <li>Diced onions 1 cup</li>
    <li>Diced green peppers 1 cup</li>
    <li>Diced yellow peppers 1 cup</li>
    <li>Vegetable oil 2tbps</li>
    <li>Diced carrots 1 cup</li>
    <li>Salt and pepper to taste </li>
    <li>Spices of your choice</li>
  </ul>
 <h2>Instructions</h2>
   <ol>
     <li>Preheat oil in sauce pan</li>
     <li>Add carrots, fry until soft.</li>
     <li>Add onions, green and yellow peppers.</li>
     <li>Add salt, pepper and spices</li>
     <li>Add precooked rice</li>
     <li>Remove from heat and serve hot</li>
   </ol>
